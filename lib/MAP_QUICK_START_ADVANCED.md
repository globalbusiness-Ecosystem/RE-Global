## ⚡ 60-Second Quick Start

### 1️⃣ Open Map (10 seconds)
- Tap "Map" icon in bottom navigation
- Wait for world map to load
- See all 49 global properties appear

### 2️⃣ Enable Market Insights (15 seconds)
- Tap filter icon (≡) at top
- Tap "Advanced Filters" button
- Toggle "Market Insights" ON
- See insights panel appear on left

### 3️⃣ Filter by ROI (20 seconds)
- Scroll in filters to "ROI Score"
- Drag slider to 80+ for best investments
- Watch properties update instantly
- Top investments highlighted

### 4️⃣ Find Your Deal (15 seconds)
- Look at Market Insights
- Click "Recommended For You"
- Check "New Listings" (hot deals)
- Tap property to see full details

**Total: 60 seconds to find investment!**

## 🎯 Step-by-Step Features Guide

### Feature 1: ROI Score Filter

**What it is:** Investment potential rating 0-100

**How to use:**
\`\`\`
1. Tap Advanced Filters (📊 icon)
2. Find "ROI Score" slider
3. Drag to desired range:
   - 90-100: Exceptional (emerging markets)
   - 80-89: Excellent (growth areas)
   - 70-79: Good (stable markets)
   - <70: Caution (mature areas)
4. Properties auto-filter
5. Only best-potential show on map
\`\`\`

**Why it matters:**
- Skip mediocre investments
- Find hidden gems fast
- Based on real market data
- Updated continuously

---

### Feature 2: Market Trend Indicators

**What they are:** Market direction signals

**Three Types:**
- 📈 **Up Arrow**: Market rising (buy signal)
- 📉 **Down Arrow**: Market falling (caution)
- 🚩 **Flag**: Market stable (safe bet)

**How to see them:**
\`\`\`
1. Tap any property on map
2. Look at "Trend" section
3. See the arrow/flag icon
4. Read what market is doing
5. Make informed decision
\`\`\`

**Strategy Tips:**
- Buy on ↑ (ride the wave)
- Hold on → (collect rent)
- Wait on ↓ (for reversal)

---

### Feature 3: Smart Recommendations

**What it is:** AI picks properties for YOU

**How it works:**
\`\`\`
1. System looks at your portfolio
2. Finds similar properties
3. BUT with HIGHER appreciation
4. Recommends top 3
5. You see them in Market Insights
\`\`\`

**Example:**
\`\`\`
Your portfolio average: 10% appreciation
System finds: Properties with 12-15% appreciation
You get: "Recommended For You" with 3 suggestions
\`\`\`

**Click to view:**
1. Open Market Insights panel
2. See "Recommended For You" section
3. Top 3 properties listed
4. Shows appreciation % for each
5. Tap any to view full details

---

### Feature 4: Market Insights Dashboard

**What it shows:**

#### A. Recommended For You
- Personalized property suggestions
- Sorted by investment quality
- Shows % appreciation
- All matching your style

#### B. Top Gainers
- Current market ROI average
- Visual strength meter
- Top 2 best properties
- Market sentiment overview

#### C. New Listings
- Properties added last 7 days
- "Hot deals" before price rises
- Days since listing shown
- Limited inventory alert

**How to use:**
\`\`\`
1. Click Market Insights toggle
2. Panel opens on left side
3. Read all three sections
4. Click any property card
5. View full details in bottom sheet
6. Make investment decision
\`\`\`
