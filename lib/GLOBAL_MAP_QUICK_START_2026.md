### 🔍 How to Find Properties

**Step 1: Choose View**
- Select Markers, Heatmap, or Clusters mode

**Step 2: Filter**
- Type: Buy, Rent, Hotel, Invest
- Price: Use slider (0-10,000π)
- Country: Select specific countries
- Region: Click region buttons

**Step 3: Sort**
- By Price: Lowest to highest
- By ROI: Best returns first
- By Trending: Bullish markets first

**Step 4: Explore**
- Click any marker for details
- See property stats and images
- Check ROI and market trend
