## 🎓 For Users

### Getting Started
1. Open the Map page
2. Click **Global Tour** to see all properties
3. Use **Regional Buttons** to focus on areas
4. Switch between **View Modes** for different insights
5. Apply **Filters** for targeted searches

### Understanding Market Sentiment
- **📈 Bullish**: More up trends = positive market
- **➡️ Neutral**: Balanced activity = stable market
- **📉 Bearish**: More down trends = cautious market

### Finding Best Investments
1. Switch to **Heatmap View**
2. Look for **Red zones** (highest opportunity)
3. Sort by **ROI** (return percentage)
4. Check **Regional Performance** for trends
5. Click properties for details
