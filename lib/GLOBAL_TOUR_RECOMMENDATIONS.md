## 🎨 Visualization Tips

### Reading the Heatmap
\`\`\`
🔵 BLUE zones = Established markets, low volatility
   - Europe, North America major cities
   - 5-8% ROI, stable growth

🟢 GREEN zones = Growing markets, moderate growth
   - Asia coastal cities, Mediterranean towns
   - 10-15% ROI, emerging opportunities

🔴 RED zones = High-growth opportunities, higher risk
   - Middle East development zones
   - Africa emerging cities
   - 15-20% ROI, frontier investments
\`\`\`

### Best Times to Enable Heatmap
- ✅ After filtering by category (get pattern)
- ✅ After selecting regions (compare sub-regions)
- ✅ When comparing ROI across similar properties
- ❌ When zoomed in >13 (too granular, disables auto)
- ❌ When comparing raw prices (not ROI-relevant)
